Blue Origin

suborbital flight

On July 20, 2021, Blue Origin's New Shepard suborbital rocket launched a capsule with people into space for the first time in the company's history, with the richest man in the world, Jeff Bezos, on board.

New Shepard, 18 m high and 4 m wide, is a reusable spacecraft. The booster unit raises the pressurized passenger ship to a height of about 76 km. The momentum propels the capsule up into space.

After that, she descends to the ground under three parachutes. The launch vehicle is also returning home.
